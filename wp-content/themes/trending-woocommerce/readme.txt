== Description ==

Trending WooCommerce is a lovely, current, and responsive eCommerce / WooCommerce Store WordPress theme. Trending WooCommerce is an ideal fit for any blog, business, portfolio, WooCommerce storefronts, normal food related sites, natural internet business site, natural food store, Natural business sectors, stores. Moreover, The theme accompanies Elementor page manufacturer on WordPress and call to action, banner and product blocks. With the demo content and video instructional exercises you can build your site rapidly and easily.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
